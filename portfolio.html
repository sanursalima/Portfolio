Project’s Team

BREE – Mental Health App (UI/UX Design)

BREE is a mental health-focused mobile application designed with a clean, minimalist interface to ensure an intuitive and calming user experience. Developed as the final project for the GDG OC program at UIN Jakarta, this app prioritizes accessibility and ease of use. I contributed as the UX Researcher, conducting user research and defining the user journey, while collaborating closely with a designer who crafted the visual elements.

VeryMindful – A Mental Health Education Website

Aligned with the goal of “Good Health and Well-being,” VeryMindful is a web-based platform designed to be a trusted source of mental health education. More than just providing information, the site aims to empower individuals to take proactive steps in caring for their mental well-being. This project was developed as the final output of HITLAB under HIMSI’s work program. I contributed as the UI designer for the website and was also responsible for developing the learning guide section.
